Your Security & Privacy Matters

At KwickLingo, we've built privacy and security into every aspect of our platform. Here's how we protect you and your conversations.

Our Security Protections

Multiple layers of protection for your peace of mind

End-to-End Encryption

All video calls and messages are encrypted so only you and the person you're communicating with can access them.

Identity Protection

We never share your personal information with other users without your explicit permission.

Secure Connections

All data is transmitted over secure TLS connections to prevent interception.

No Call Recording

We don't record or store your video calls. What happens in your conversations stays between you.

Strict Moderation

Our AI and human moderation team works 24/7 to keep the platform safe and appropriate.

Data Minimization

We only collect the minimum data needed to provide our service and delete it when no longer needed.

Military-Grade Encryption

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption to protect all your communications. This is the same level of security used by banks and governments worldwide.

Your messages are encrypted before they leave your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient.

Security FAQs

Common questions about our security practices

Does KwickLingo store my video calls?

No, we do not record or store your video calls. All calls are peer-to-peer when possible and are never saved on our servers.

What personal information do you collect?

We only collect the minimum information needed to provide our service, such as your device information for technical support and approximate location for matching. You can choose what profile information to share.

How do you verify user identities?

We use multiple verification methods including phone number verification and optional photo verification. All verified users receive a badge on their profile.

Can I use KwickLingo anonymously?

Yes, you can use a nickname and don't need to provide personal details. However, verified accounts get access to more features.

How can I report inappropriate behavior?

You can report users directly during or after a call through the app. All reports are reviewed by our moderation team within 24 hours.
